Our Founders

Marketing

Tracy Jackson is a fashion merchandiser who has been in the business for more than 15 years. She has a keen eye for style and trends, and she knows how to create appealing and profitable displays for various products. She is happy to be a part of the Nina Jones team, where she can apply her skills and experience to a new and exciting challenge.



Bianca So is a dynamic and accomplished advertising professional who has a wealth of experience in different roles in the industry. She earned a master’s degree in advertising from the University of Texas at Austin, where she excelled in both academic and practical aspects of the field. She now works as a brand manager at Nina Jones, a fashion boutique that offers stylish and affordable clothing and accessories for women. Bianca is in charge of planning and executing marketing campaigns that promote the brand’s image and products across various media platforms.



Gina Wendell is a proficient and experienced marketing manager who has been working in the field for over a decade. She has a master’s degree in marketing from Louisiana State University, where she graduated with honors and gained valuable skills and knowledge in the field. She currently works as the head of marketing at Nina Jones, a fashion boutique that sells trendy and affordable clothing and accessories for women. Gina is responsible for overseeing and implementing marketing strategies that increase the brand’s awareness, sales, and customer loyalty.

Our Team

"Creativity is the spark that ignites the flame of teamwork. Together, we can achieve more than we ever could alone."

Nina Jones is a fashion boutique that celebrates the beauty and diversity of women. It was founded in 2011 by three friends who met in 2009 while on vacation in Paris, the capital of fashion and culture. Tracy, Bianca and Gina shared a common love of fashion and a dream of creating their own personal fashion brand that would reflect their identities and values. Nina Jones offers a curated selection of clothing and accessories for women who want to express their individuality and confidence. Nina Jones caters to women of all ages, sizes and styles, and provides them with a variety of options for every occasion and mood. Nina Jones also offers personalized styling services and consultations for customers who want to explore new trends or find the perfect outfit for a special event. Nina Jones helps women discover and embrace their own style and beauty. Nina Jones is more than just a fashion boutique. It is a community of women who support each other and share their passion for fashion. Tracy, Bianca and Gina hired a team of dedicated women who have the skills and enthusiasm to bring their vision to life. They value teamwork, collaboration and mutual respect, and they treat their customers like friends. Nina Jones is a place where women can find not only fashionable clothes and accessories, but also inspiration, joy and happiness. Nina Jones is a fashion boutique that makes women feel empowered, beautiful and happy.
